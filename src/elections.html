The presidential primary will be held Tuesday, April 28th, 2020. The deadline to change party enrollment is now February 14th, 2020. For more information about deadlines and the political calendar see the links to the left.

Other changes to New York State election law (effective going forward) include:

  • New law relating to allowing employee time off from work for the purpose of voting (details here).
  • New requirement for ballot access related to filings by mail or overnight delivery service (details here).
  • Campaign finance rules have been updated relating to contributions by LLCs (details here).
  • Voter registrations now move among counties for New York State's voters, rather than starting a new registration with each move across a county line.
  • Early voting will occur at our Canton Board of Elections Office (address here) in the two weeks before each upcoming special, primary, or general election.
  • Pre-registration, previously available to those of age 17, is extended to age 16 effective January 2020.

As more changes in election law are enacted, your county Board of Elections will keep you informed; however, please call, visit or email us with your questions.
