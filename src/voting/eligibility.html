Voter Registration Eligiblity

In order to be eligible to vote in St. Lawrence County, you must meet the following requirements:

  1. Be a United States citizen.
  2. Be 18 years old by December 31st of the year in which you file your registration form, and be 18 years old by the date of the general, primary, or other election in which you want to vote.
  3. Be a resident of St. Lawrence County for at least 30 days prior to the election in which you want to vote.
  4. Not be in prison for a felony conviction, or on parole for a felony conviction, unless parolee pardoned or restored rights of citizenship.
  5. Not be adjudged mentally incompetent by a court.
  6. Not be claiming the right to vote elsewhere.

Any duly registered voter is eligible to vote in a General Election, and in a Primary Election voters who are enrolled in the party that is holding the Primary are eligible.
